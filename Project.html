Nike

Nike, is one of the world’s leading sportswear brands, renowned for its innovative athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment. Headquartered in Beaverton, Oregon, Nike has become a symbol of performance, style, and global influence in the sports industry.Over the years, Nike has built a strong presence through high-profile endorsements, cutting-edge product design, and a commitment to sustainability. The company continually pushes the boundaries of sports innovation, making it a dominant force in athletic culture worldwide.
















Adidas

Adidas, is a global leader in sportswear, known for its high-performance athletic footwear, apparel, and accessories. With its iconic three-stripes logo, Adidas has established itself as a key player in the sports and fashion industries. The brand is recognized for blending innovation with classic design, offering products that cater to both professional athletes and everyday consumers. Adidas has also emphasized sustainability, incorporating eco-friendly materials into its products.














Converse

Converse, is an iconic American footwear brand best known for its timeless Chuck Taylor All-Star sneakers. Initially designed as basketball shoes, Converse has evolved into a symbol of casual style, embraced by athletes, musicians, and fashion enthusiasts alike. The brand’s simple yet distinctive design, featuring a rubber sole and canvas upper, has remained largely unchanged for decades, contributing to its enduring popularity.
